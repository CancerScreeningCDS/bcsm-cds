description: Refer for breast biopsy. Initial modality will typically be image-guided core needle biopsy (guided by mammography, ultrasound, or MRI). In certain conditions, an initial excisional biopsy may be preferable. These may include lesions anatomically unsuitable for needle biopsy or patient anatomic considerations.
