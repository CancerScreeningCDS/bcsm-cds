HighRiskAssessment

title: Perform 5 year risk assessment

description: A 5 year risk assessment, either informal or formal using a validated instrument, will help determine if the patient may benefit from referral for high risk breast specialty evaluation. Women at higher than average 5 year risk may be candidates for referral to breast specialist to discuss primary risk reduction interventions. These may include medications to reduce risk (chemoprevention), risk reducing surgery or enhanced surveillance.
